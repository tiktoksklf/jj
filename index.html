How Jonathan Passed

On February 25, 2025, Jonathan tragically lost his life in a motorcycle accident. At approximately 7:34 a.m., he was riding his Honda motorcycle southbound on U.S. 19 when a northbound SUV attempted to turn left at Windward Boulevard, crossing into his path. The impact was severe, and despite immediate response, Jonathan sustained fatal injuries and was pronounced dead at the scene. The driver of the SUV suffered minor injuries. His passing leaves a void in the hearts of his family, friends, and all who knew him.
