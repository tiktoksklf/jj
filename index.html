In Loving Memory of Jonathan Terry Judd

Born: April 28, 2004 – Passed: February 25, 2025

How Jonathan Passed

On February 25, 2025, Jonathan tragically lost his life in a motorcycle accident. At approximately 7:34 a.m., he was riding his Honda motorcycle southbound on U.S. 19 when a northbound SUV turned left into his path. Despite immediate response, Jonathan sustained fatal injuries and was pronounced dead at the scene. His passing leaves a void in the hearts of his family, friends, and all who knew him.
